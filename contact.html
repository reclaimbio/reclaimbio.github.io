Frequently Asked Questions

Each ReClaim BioPod is designed for a specific type of water system:

  • Use the Aerobic System Polishing BioPod if you have an aerobic treatment unit and want to polish effluent before it's sprayed or dispersed into soil.
  • Choose the Septic Field Saver BioPod to protect your traditional septic drainfield from clogging and extend its lifespan.
  • Select the Rainwater Runoff Polisher BioPod if you're filtering stormwater before it enters a drain, creek, or recharge area.

Still unsure? Contact us and we'll help match you with the right product.

Most BioPods last 6 to 12 months, depending on water quality, flow rate, and system use. We recommend checking for reduced flow or performance seasonally and replacing media annually for best results.
