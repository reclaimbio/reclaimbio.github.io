Aerobic Systems

Aerobic System Polishing BioPod

Our Aerobic System Polishing BioPod is designed to filter treated effluent before soil dispersal, ensuring that water released into the environment is cleaner and safer.

Key Features:

  • Removes remaining contaminants from aerobic treatment unit effluent
  • Extends the life of soil dispersal fields
  • Reduces environmental impact of treated wastewater
  • Available in lengths from 2 to 10 feet to match your system's flow rate

Technical Specifications:

Material: Schedule 80 4" PVC
Filter Media: Mesquite chips & biochar
Sizes: 2', 4', 6', 8', 10'
Flow Rate: 5-50 GPD per foot

Installation:

The Aerobic System Polishing BioPod is installed after your aerobic treatment unit and before final soil dispersal. It can be installed horizontally or vertically depending on your system configuration.

Septic Systems

Septic Field Saver BioPod

The Septic Field Saver BioPod protects your drainfield by polishing septic tank outflow, extending the life of your septic system and preventing costly repairs.

Key Features:

  • Captures solids and organic matter before they reach your drainfield
  • Prevents drainfield clogging and failure
  • Extends septic system lifespan by years
  • Easy to maintain with replaceable filter media

Technical Specifications:

Material: Schedule 80 4" PVC
Filter Media: Mesquite chips & biochar
Sizes: 4', 6', 8', 10'
Flow Rate: 150-500 GPD

Installation:

The Septic Field Saver BioPod is installed between your septic tank and drainfield. It can be integrated into new systems or retrofitted to existing septic systems to extend their life.

Stormwater Management

Rainwater Runoff Polisher BioPod

Our Rainwater Runoff Polisher BioPod filters stormwater before it enters the environment, reducing pollutants in our waterways and protecting aquatic ecosystems.

Key Features:

  • Captures oils, heavy metals, and other pollutants from rainwater runoff
  • Ideal for parking lots, driveways, and other impervious surfaces
  • Helps meet stormwater management regulations
  • Scalable for residential to commercial applications

Technical Specifications:

Material: Schedule 80 4" PVC
Filter Media: Mesquite chips & biochar
Sizes: 1', 2', 4', 6'
Flow Rate: Varies by application

Installation:

The Rainwater Runoff Polisher BioPod can be installed in various configurations depending on your specific needs. It works well in rain gardens, bioswales, and other stormwater management systems.
