How ReClaim BioPods Work

Natural Materials

Our BioPods use locally sourced mesquite wood chips and biochar created using a sustainable 55-gallon drum method.

Biological Filtration

BioPods create the perfect environment for beneficial microorganisms that break down contaminants naturally.

Sustainable & Compostable

When filtration media is spent, it can be composted, returning valuable carbon and nutrients to the soil.
